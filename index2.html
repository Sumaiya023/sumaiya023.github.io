Developed an agent-based model in Python (Mesa) to simulate measles outbreaks across all counties in the US using MMR vaccination coverage and population data. The model estimates the number of susceptible, infected, and recovered individuals per county, and outputs color-coded risk classifications.

Tools & Data

  • Languages/Libraries: Python (Mesa, Pandas, OpenPyXL, GeoPandas, Folium)
  • Data Sources: Arizona Department of Health Services (MMR), U.S. Census (Population), TIGER/Line Shapefiles

What I Did

  1. Collected & cleaned MMR and population data
  2. Designed and implemented an agent-based measles simulation
  3. Classified counties by outbreak risk and visualized using Folium & Excel
